About Us

A start-up small business coffee shop inception at Burwood NSW owned by a little family of 3 named Raf, Precious & Nafeezah, inspired by their daughter Nafeezah which they created to generate savings for her future.

It's a mobile coffee shop, using a small cart/truck roaming around Burwood area. They also offer sweets and pastry. Their main best seller coffee is the Kapeng Barako, originated from Batangas Philippines. They also have Sagada Coffee from Baguio City, Red Bourbon, Yellow Bourbon from CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION, Arabica from Visayas Region. For food, they have creme puff baked by Rafcious, ham & cheese croissant & egg bacon served all day. They bring Filipino taste here in Australia to share in this region.
